Why Drowsiness Detection Matters ?

Every year, drowsy driving causes thousands of accidents worldwide. Fatigue reduces reaction time, impairs judgment, and increases the risk of road mishaps. Our AI-powered solution helps detect signs of drowsiness early to prevent accidents before they happen!

⚙️ How It Works

1. The camera detects facial features.

2. AI analyzes eye movements and head position.

3. If drowsiness is detected, an alert is triggered.
